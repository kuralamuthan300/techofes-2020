ALCATRAZ

Have what it takes to make it out of Alcatraz? Put your puzzle solving skills to the test, go through rounds of analytic, aptitude questionnaires, mini games and be deemed the Alcatraz champion and the most likely to escape Alcatraz!



HOW STUFF WORKS

How stuff works event deals with testing the knowledge of the participants in the domain of science and machines ranging from submarines, aircrafts to common household gadgets. Questions will be asked on working concepts, terminology and mechanisms of various machines. It provides participants an insight into the way many things work.


SONG MARATHON

Song marathan is a fun game to relax your mind and enjoy the moment . Are you a crazy about songs? Then here is a chance to prove your knowledge in lyric (Tamil songs) with fun. This event will give you some new, ridiculous and wonderful experience. This event is all about pen and paper where the first round will be fill the blanks and second round is about translate the songs given.


THE TRIWIZARD TOURNAMENT

HP series has long been fascinating the young Indian readers’ minds with the spellbinding place of Hogwarts, the alchemy and magic happening around. So, how much do you know about the boy who lived? How well you have travelled along with the “Trio”? Come, compete with fellow Potter heads and emerge as their undisputed “Elder”. Wingardium Quiziiosa!!!

DEATH NOTE

The real evil is the power to kill people!! Love to hunt those evils?? Get ready to be a Kira, because no one knows when will the death note drops on this rotten world !! Run and chase to be a light of this world of darkness !!



FRIEND-O-METER

“A true friend is the greatest of all blessings and that which we take least care of all to acquire.” Its quite rare to find someone who sees the same world you see. Do you think you are one among them? This event is a fun game which has a set of questions about your friend. In second round, some fun games will be conducted and the team which has the highest score will be rewarded.


SHERLOCK HOLMES

According to a survey taken by a secretive detective agency, approximately 70% of the population are basically detective. Some of them are born detectives while some are trained detectives and some are catalysed detectives. So, which type do you belong to? Participate to find out.
