INTRA VARIETY

Nothing is pleasant that is not spiced with variety.! Truely we need variety in most of the aspects. Food, dress, ornaments, etc. It not only breaks the monotonousness, rather it exaggerates the essence of things. Here we have several crews to entertain us for our whole time with their Variety show.! Get entertained by creativity, comedy, sentiment, dance, song and the kith and kin of ‘ART’ in one stage.
